About
A fan-made mod of BFDIA 5b featuring The Stupid Trailer lyrics. Powered by Ruffle.
Files needed
Place 5b.swf and levels.txt in the same folder as this HTML file.
Controls
Arrow keys / WASD · Z / Space · R to restart · P to pause
